Sheet pan tacos

Ingredients:

  1. 2 tablespoons olive oil, plus more if needed
  2. 1 pound ground beef
  3. 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  4. 4 ounces diced green chiles
  5. 1 teaspoon chili powder
  6. 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  7. 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  8. 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  9. 10 corn tortillas
  10. 1 cup refried beans (optional)
  11. 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  12. toppings: cilantro, sour cream, salsa, guacamole or hot sauce (optional)
